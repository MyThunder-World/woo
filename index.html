About Kamaljith

My journey in music began with a simple curiosity about sound and evolved into a passion for creating immersive audio experiences. As a music creator and producer, I blend traditional composition with modern production techniques to craft unique soundscapes.

As the founder of Skela Media Works, I lead a creative collective focused on elevating artistic vision through comprehensive workshops and community-driven projects. My work spans across multiple genres, from ambient soundscapes to dynamic electronic compositions.
